Senior Backend Engineer — DataFlow Inc.

About the Role
We're looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our platform team. You'll design and own core services that power our real-time data ingestion and analytics product used by 500+ enterprise customers.

Responsibilities
- Design, build, and maintain high-throughput microservices in Python
- Lead technical design discussions and mentor junior engineers
- Build and operate data pipelines processing millions of events per day
- Drive reliability improvements: observability, alerting, incident response
- Collaborate with ML and frontend teams to ship product features end to end

Requirements
- 5+ years of professional software engineering experience
- Strong Python skills; experience with async frameworks (FastAPI, aiohttp) preferred
- Experience designing and operating distributed systems at scale
- Proficiency with PostgreSQL or similar relational databases
- Familiarity with cloud infrastructure (AWS preferred): EC2, S3, RDS, EKS
- Experience with containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Solid understanding of CI/CD practices

Nice to Have
- Experience with Kafka or other event streaming systems
- Prior tech lead or mentorship experience
- Familiarity with dbt or data transformation tooling
- Experience with Terraform or infrastructure-as-code

We Offer
Competitive salary ($160k–$200k), equity, full remote, unlimited PTO.
